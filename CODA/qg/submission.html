AAAI Symposium on Question Generation

November 4-6, 2011 in Arlington, VA, USA

Final paper submissions: September 9, 2011 ( formatting instructions).

Submission site will be active later in August. AAAI will send detailed submission instructions.

NOTE: A permission to distribute form must be signed by one of the authors and submitted by fax(650-321-4457), or email (fss11@aaai.org), AND the original signed form should be mailed to AAAI office (to be received before September 9, 2011). Please note the name of the symposium (Question Generation) and the title of your paper at the top of the form.

COMING SOON: Submission page and instruction will be available on the AAAI website

Submission of extended abstract: May 20, 2011

We will be accepting extended abstracts for QG 2011, between 1500-2000 words for long papers, and 800-1000 words for short papers. We welcome submissions describing work in progress and intended research plans. Abstracts must be submitted as PDF files. No styles are specified for the submission at this time.

Submission to QG 2011 should be done through the EasyChair system. Authors who do not have accounts on EasyChair will be prompted to set up a new account before they can make their submission.

The full papers (up to 8 pages, including references) and short papers (up to 4 pages, including references) for poster and/or oral presentations are due on September 9. Presenters of system implementations will be given the option of presenting their work as a demo, a poster, or a combination of both.

Submission questions should be directed to the organizers at qg2011 at googlegroups dot com

Type of Posting:  Preliminary Call for Interest: 2011 AAAI Fall Symposium on Question Generation, please RSVP

The organizing committee of the 4th workshop on Question Generation is seeking feedback on the level of interest for the event to be held as part of the AAAI Fall Symposium Series, Friday - Sunday, November 4-6, in Arlington, Virginia.

The workshop will serve as an interdisciplinary forum for a diverse community of researchers from Artificial Intelligence, Natural Language Processing, Cognitive Science, and Psychology, working on theories of question generation and its applications. This workshop will follow three previous workshops (http://www.questiongeneration.org): the 3rd Workshop on Question Generation held in conjunction with the 10th International Conference on Intelligent Tutoring Systems in 2010, the 2nd Workshop on Question Generation held in conjunction with the 2009 International Conference on Artificial Intelligence in Education, and the 2008 NSF Workshop on the Question Generation Shared Task and Evaluation Challenge.

AAAI symposia is a bi-annual event hosting symposia on a range of topics related to Artificial Intelligence. Attendees of the symposium participate in any number of exciting events. To see the list of symposia from previous years, please see: http://www.aaai.org/Symposia/Fall/fall-symposia.php

At this time, the committee is gathering an estimate of the "potential" workshop attendance if it is held at the Fall 2011 AAAI Symposia. Please send email expressing your potential interest for participation to s.stoyanchev@open.ac.uk, including your name, institutional affiliation, email. Additional suggestions for potential participants are welcome.
